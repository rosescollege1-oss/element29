Common Questions

What is your travel policy?

Free within 35 miles of Malvern. Beyond that, it's a flat $100 + $0.50/mile. Simple and transparent.

How do I secure my date?

A 30% non-refundable deposit is required via Square. The remaining balance is due by noon on your event day.

Are you insured?

Yes, we carry general liability and liquor liability insurance to protect you and your venue throughout the event.

Do you provide the alcohol?

As a "dry hire" service, we cannot legally purchase or sell alcohol. You purchase the spirits, and we handle everything else!
