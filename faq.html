Common Questions

Do you provide alcohol?

No. Element 29 provides bartending services only. The client is responsible for purchasing and supplying all alcohol. We provide a detailed alcohol shopping list and are happy to review your purchase plan to ensure quantities are correct.

What do you provide?

We provide our copper trailer mobile bar, professional bartenders, ice, mixers, garnishes, cups, napkins straws, bar equipment, 2 kegs, full setup and breakdown, and custom drink menu planning assistance!

How many bartenders will we have?

Element 29 staffs at a ratio of one bartender per 50 guests to ensure smooth, efficient service throughout your event. Because every celebration is unique, we may recommend additional staffing when appropriate to deliver an exceptional guest experience.

What are your travel policies?

Travel within 35 miles of Malvern, Arkansas is included in all packages. For events beyond that radius, a flat fee plus a per-mile charge will be applied to your estimate.

What is required to book?

To secure your date, we require a signed service agreement and a 30% non-refundable deposit. The remaining balance is due 1 day prior to your event.

What happens if the weather is bad?

For outdoor events, clients must provide a weather contingency plan. In cases of severe weather or unsafe conditions, Element 29 reserves the right to pause or discontinue service for safety.
