Cocktails

Electric Airhead

A bold, electric mix of blueberry vodka, bright lemonade, and blue curaçao with a splash of sweet & sour for the perfect tang. Finished with a sour powder rim for a nostalgic candy-like kick. Also available as a Refresher — all the flavors you love with Red Bull for a late-night boost.

Signature Margarita

A refined twist on the classic. Smooth reposado tequila meets fresh lime juice and triple sec, balanced with agave for just the right touch of sweetness. Served with a salted rim and fresh lime garnish — crisp, clean, and effortlessly timeless.

Spicy Margarita

Bold, vibrant, and not for the faint of heart. Reposado tequila infused with jalapeño heat is muddled fresh with sliced jalapeños, shaken with lime juice and triple sec, and finished with a Tajín rim for a citrus-spice kick. Smooth at first sip — with just the right slow burn.

Skinny Margarita

Clean, crisp, and refreshingly simple. Reposado tequila meets fresh lime juice and triple sec with no added sweetener — letting the bright citrus and smooth tequila shine through. Light, balanced, and effortlessly refreshing.

Strawberry Shortcake

Sweet, creamy, and just a little indulgent. Whipped vodka blends with cranberry juice for a soft berry finish, topped with whipped cream and served with a graham cracker rim and fresh strawberry garnish. Dessert in a glass.

Yaeger Bomb

A high-energy classic. Sugar-free Red Bull meets chilled Jägermeister for a smooth, bold drop that keeps the celebration alive. Simple. Strong. Straight to the point.

Copper Cauldron

A rich, smooth blend of Four Roses Small Batch and Maker’s Mark bourbon, brightened with fresh lemon juice and honey syrup. Finished with a splash of root beer for a subtle caramel warmth and signature copper glow.

Mojito

Bright, crisp, and endlessly refreshing. Fresh mint and lime are gently muddled and shaken with smooth rum, then topped with sparkling soda for a clean, citrus-forward finish. Light, cool, and perfect for warm evenings. Also available in Coconut & Blackberry flavors.

Old Fashioned

Timeless and refined. Smooth bourbon stirred with sugar and bitters, finished with a citrus peel for subtle warmth and depth. Simple. Strong. Classic.

Moscow Mule

Vodka, fresh lime juice, and spicy ginger beer served ice-cold for a crisp, zesty finish. Clean, refreshing, and always a crowd favorite.

Aperol Spritz

Bright and bubbly with a bittersweet citrus edge. Aperol blended with prosecco and topped with soda creates a light, refreshing cocktail perfect for golden hour celebrations.

Lemon Drop

Crisp vodka shaken with fresh lemon juice and triple sec, served with a sugared rim for the perfect balance of sweet and tart. Smooth, bright, and effortlessly fun.

Blackberry Bourbon Smash

Bold bourbon muddled with fresh blackberries and lemon, lightly sweetened and shaken to perfection. Rich, slightly tart, and beautifully balanced — a rustic classic with a modern edge.

Mocktails

Coconut Nojito

A tropical twist on the classic mojito. Fresh lime and cool mint are muddled together and blended with creamy coconut and sparkling soda for a light, refreshing drink that tastes like a beach vacation in a glass.

Cherry Punch

Bright, juicy cherry meets citrus and a splash of sparkling soda to create a bold, fruity punch that’s sweet, refreshing, and irresistibly vibrant.

Sparkling Tropical

Pineapple, mango, and citrus juices come together with chilled sparkling water for a crisp tropical refresher bursting with sunshine-bright flavor.

Cherry Hibiscus Refresher

Tart cherry and floral hibiscus combine with fresh lemon and sparkling water for a beautifully deep red drink that’s both tangy and refreshing.

Watermelon Basil Cooler

Fresh watermelon juice blended with fragrant basil and a squeeze of lime, topped with sparkling soda for a crisp, garden-fresh refresher.

Cranberry Lime Fizz

Tart cranberry and bright lime are balanced with a hint of sweetness and finished with sparkling soda for a perfectly refreshing citrus fizz.

Honey Orangeade

Fresh-squeezed orange and lemon juices sweetened with golden honey and topped with chilled soda water for a smooth, citrusy drink that’s both bright and comforting.

Mermaid Refresher

A vibrant ocean-blue refresher made with tropical citrus, coconut, and sparkling lemonade for a fun, island-inspired drink that’s as beautiful as it is refreshing.
